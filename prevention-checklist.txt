Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your OTP, passwords, or banking details with anyone
2. Always verify website URLs before entering sensitive information
3. Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links in emails or messages
4. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
5. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
6. Regularly check your bank transactions for any unusual activity
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
8. Install apps only from trusted sources like official app stores
9. Keep your devices updated with the latest security updates
10. Report cyber fraud immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
11. Call the cybercrime helpline number 1930 in case of financial fraud
