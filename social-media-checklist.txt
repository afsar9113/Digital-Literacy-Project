Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

Do's:
1. Maintain a professional and respectful profile
2. Think before posting any content
3. Use privacy settings to protect personal information
4. Share useful and informative content
5. Respect others' opinions and communicate politely

Don'ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
2. Do not post offensive or inappropriate content
3. Do not spread fake news or misinformation
4. Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling
5. Do not overshare unnecessary personal details
